Typefaces

2026

An exploration of type and iconography through a daily emoji-making practice using glyphs from a single typeface. After 30 days, I compiled the emojis into a catalog that showcases how they can function within visual culture by inserting them into found Getty Images and everyday scenarios.

House of Yes Club Rebrand

2025

A collaborative rebrand of Brooklyn’s iconic nightlife venue rooted in the club’s DIY origins and expressive spirit. The identity system embraces fluidity and experimentation through a modular visual language inspired by the ever-changing energy of the space.

Redesigning Bus Stops

2024

A speculative redesign of NYC bus stops that replaces traditional hard benches with playground-inspired swing seating to create a more playful and engaging public waiting space. The project explores how everyday infrastructure can introduce moments of stimulation and joy into daily routines while also serving as an introduction to Rhino 8 and 3D printing.
