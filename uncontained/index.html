What happens when there’s nowhere to pee in NYC?

Public Restrooms Across NYC

In a city of over eight million people, finding a public restroom shouldn’t feel impossible. Each dot represents a public bathroom recorded by NYC Open Data parks, transit hubs, and community spaces.

But when you zoom in, you’ll notice how few and far between these points are. For many New Yorkers, there’s simply nowhere to go.

If you explore further, you'll notice that a lot of the public restrooms aren't even in operation!
